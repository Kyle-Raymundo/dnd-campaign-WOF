Player Characters

Unravel (formerly Wayfarer)

A chimera of Kenku memory and Water Genasi rewriting. Once Wayfarer, now Unravel — a bridge between the drowned skies and the rewritten tides. His destiny is neither inherited nor abandoned: it is carved anew.

Tamarrind

A soul stitched by faith and memory, torn between the Spiral Fang's fading light and the dangerous promises of the forgotten gods. Carrying the burden of lost scriptures and the last flickers of his original self.

Slag

A High Elf wanderer hollowed by endless recursion. Slag pieces together what was lost, but each answer only births deeper questions. In the ruins of memory, he searches for the shape of who he once was — or who he could become.

Main Allies

Bartending Bob

A simulacrum crafted from broken prayers and fading memories. Bob tends the tavern at the heart of Hollowmere, holding fast to what fragments of truth remain — even as the world forgets around him.

Velgrin

Patron Saint of Poor Decisions. Gunslinger, dramatist, and last hero of a world that refuses to remember its own mistakes. Velgrin teaches that even doomed rebellions deserve a standing ovation.

Wiels Rider

Once a butler to Queen Belabronta, now a broken cleric in a clockwork wheelchair. Wiels rolls through drowned timelines, offering prayers to gods who no longer listen — and sometimes answers born of silence.

Zamir

Paladin-Warlock hybrid. High Major Commodore of the Lethal Company. A man stitched together from broken promises and stubborn faith, dragging a trickster god's laughter behind him like a battered shield.

Nyrah of the Ninth Thread

One of the last walkers between timelines. Her world drowned, her god wore too many faces, but she still tugs gently on the threads of destiny — even if the loom itself forgot she was ever there.

Refuge Survivors

Mudge

First of the Fallen Fang who didn't get back up. Grinning madly, Mudge still hears bubbles of laughter rising from blood — and sometimes answers when no one else dares.

Kovari Emberbrand

Scholar of the Arcane Brotherhood. She documents the last dying sparks of magic as the world unwrites itself, refusing worship but recording every answer that still flickers back.

Spud

Once a proud Fang knight, now a broken relic of the Spiral. His laughter echoes strangely across the coral-lit chambers of the refuge, half memory, half warning.

Esmeralda

A tiefling squire spirited away from Hollowmere during the chaos. Bright-eyed and stubborn, unaware she is the spark that could ignite a thousand collapsing stories.

Adversaries and Shadows

Vareth-Khul (formerly "Mulch")

The quiet architect behind the rewriting of Hollowmere. Once a peer of the Spiral Fang, now a thief of memory, a butcher of timelines, and a god in waiting.

The Bubble Sage

Once a guardian of joy and memory among the Bubbleborn. Twisted by sorrow and betrayal, now only a broken echo remains, spiraling into the deep abysses of the forgotten.

The Hallowed Warden of the Abyss

Silent and remorseless, this guardian of the collapsing shrine wields the echoes of faith like weapons. Neither alive nor dead — only a memory too stubborn to release its grip on the drowned world.

Others Lost or Forgotten

Lyra Brightsong

A bard who fell into Hollowmere and tried to bring music back to the ruined streets. Erased by Vareth with a whisper, she remains only as a tremor in memory — a song no longer sung.

The Bobs of the Blanetarium Archive

Reflections of forgotten lives. Memory-keepers stitched together from old names and broken prayers. In their echoing laughter, the players glimpsed what was lost — and what might still be found.
